# Dictionary Data Notice

`public/slowa.txt`, `public/hasla.txt`, `public/answer-metadata.json`, and `public/opening-moves.json` are generated by `npm run dictionary:build`.

Sources:

- SJP.PL game dictionary, `sjp-20260601.zip`, https://sjp.pl/sl/growy/
  - The SJP download page lists the game dictionary under GPL 2 and Creative Commons Attribution 4.0 International.
- KWJP100 frequency lists, `kwjp100-slowa-orth_lc-all.csv.gz`, https://github.com/ipipan/kwjp100-varia/tree/main/freqlists
- PoliMorf morphological dictionary, `PoliMorf-0.6.7.tab.gz`, https://zil.ipipan.waw.pl/PoliMorf
  - PoliMorf is distributed under the BSD 2-Clause license.

Generation policy:

- `slowa.txt` contains unique normalized five-letter entries from SJP.
- `hasla.txt` contains the top 6000 entries from the intersection of SJP five-letter entries and KWJP frequency rows, selected by descending ARF and written deterministically.
- `answer-metadata.json` marks answer words that appear in PoliMorf only as inflected forms of other lemmas.
- `opening-moves.json` contains the precomputed exact opening rankings for candidate-only moves and all allowed guesses for the full and likely-only answer profiles.
- No profanity or offensive-word blocklist is applied.
